Legal analysis: Art.32§2γ of Law 4412/2016 allows emergency without tender for "κατεπείγουσα ανάγκη οφειλόμενη σε απρόβλεπτη περίσταση" (urgent need caused by unforeseeable circumstances). A cleaning contract renewed every 6 months for 7 consecutive years is neither "urgent" (it is predictable months in advance) nor "unforeseeable" (it is the termination of the previous contract — a perfectly foreseeable event). The applicable Directive 2014/24/EU Art.32(2)(c) requires the circumstances to be "not attributable to the contracting authority" — repeated failure to tender in advance is attributable to the administration. ΑΧΕΠΑ's use of this procedure is structurally unlawful.
