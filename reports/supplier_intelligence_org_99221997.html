Feed Structure
ΓΝ ΒΕΝΙΖΕΛΕΙΟ uses ΚΑΤΑΚΥΡΩΣΗ (evaluation-of-qualification-documents decisions + final awards combined) as its primary procurement transparency mechanism. The hospital Board publishes each evaluation step in the competitive tender process under this decision type. Subjects consistently follow the pattern "Αξιολόγηση Δικαιολογητικών [Κατακύρωσης/Συμμετοχής]" — Greek procurement law requires each evaluation step to be individually published on Diavgeia.

This is NOT an emergency procurement burst — all 32 are competitive tender evaluation/award decisions. Pattern: Greek public hospitals typically batch-finalize multiple competitive tenders in Q4 (Oct–Dec) before year-end budget deadlines. No individual award exceeds €1M. Assessment: administrative seasonality, not a corruption signal.
