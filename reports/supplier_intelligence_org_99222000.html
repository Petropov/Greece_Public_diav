T5 — Emergency procurement overuse (Art.32§2γ): Fires when an organisation repeatedly classifies procurement as "emergency" to bypass competitive tender. The legal test is "κατεπείγουσα ανάγκη οφειλόμενη σε απρόβλεπτη περίσταση" — urgency caused by unforeseeable circumstances. A hospital needing food services in 2 months is unambiguously foreseeable.
Counting methodology: T5 reports 103 high-value emergency findings from 351 total emergency decisions. Each unique emergency contract generates multiple decisions (Προσφυγή initiation + Επικύρωση board ratification(s)). The 28 Προσφυγή/Έγκριση initiation decisions listed below represent approximately the actual unique emergency contracts.
