T9D context for Greek hospitals: In Greek public healthcare, many medical supplies, laboratory tests, and consultancy services are procured at nationally standardised prices set by the Ministry of Health or ΕΠΥ price catalogs. The presence of identical amounts across multiple suppliers is often consistent with catalog pricing, not coordination. The T9D findings at ΓΝ ΤΡΙΚΑΛΑ — all below €6,000 per occurrence — are consistent with standard catalog-priced supply contracts and are lower-priority signals.
