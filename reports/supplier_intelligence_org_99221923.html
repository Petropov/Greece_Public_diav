Use case — Emergency procurement overuse (Art.32§2γ): The emergency negotiated procedure allows skipping publication and competition when a need is genuinely unforeseeable and urgent. A service renewed as "emergency" year after year for a predictable recurring need is not an emergency — it is a legal workaround. Fires when any single award exceeds €60k on emergency grounds (double the Art.118 direct-award ceiling), or when the annual emergency rate exceeds 15%.

Use case — Price ratchet / amendment inflation: Consecutive direct awards to the same supplier within 18 months at ≤5% price change. The price from the previous contract is administratively carried forward rather than re-tested against market rates. In the stronger variant, a ΣΥΜΒΑΣΗ amendment raises the contract price mid-term, and the next award then starts at the inflated amount as the new baseline.
