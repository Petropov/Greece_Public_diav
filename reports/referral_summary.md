## How to read this document

Each finding below is rated by **evidence quality**:
- 🔴 **Primary source confirmed** — decision PDFs fetched, ADAs verified, amounts cross-checked
- 🟡 **API data, not PDF-verified** — counts and amounts from Diavgeia structured fields; subject to data-entry quality
- 🟢 **Negative / exculpatory** — examined and found no anomaly

**Referral bodies:**
- **ΕΑΑΔΗΣΥ** (Εθνική Αρχή Διαφάνειας) — administrative irregularities, direct-award threshold violations
- **ΕΣ** (Ελεγκτικό Συνέδριο) — public expenditure audit, financial irregularity
- **ΑΕΠΠ** (Αρχή Εξέτασης Προδικαστικών Προσφυγών) — procurement procedure complaints
- **Εισαγγελέας Δημοσίου** — criminal referral (fraud, breach of duty)
- **ΕΔΑ** (Επιθεώρηση Δημόσιας Διοίκησης) — disciplinary proceedings against officials

## ENTITY 1: ΓΝ Λαμίας (General Hospital)
*org=99221923 · 2020–May 2026 · 407 competitive awards vs 1,991 at peer ΓΝ Τρικάλων*

### Finding H1 — UNISON cleaning contract: late publication 🔴
| | |
|---|---|
| **What** | Award to UNISON Facility Services (ADA `ΨΠΗΓ4690ΒΜ-ΛΚ8`) issued 2 July 2025, published on Diavgeia 2 November 2025 — **4-month delay** |
| **Legal basis** | Art. 3 § 3 Law 3861/2010: publication within 15 days of signing is mandatory |
| **Amount** | €680,859 (5-month period) → annualised ~€1.634M/year |
| **Referral to** | **ΕΑΑΔΗΣΥ** (administrative breach); **ΕΣ** (pre-audit of public contract) |
| **Supporting ADAs** | `ΨΠΗΓ4690ΒΜ-ΛΚ8` (award Jul 2025, published Nov 2025) |
| **Context** | UNISON has held this contract since Oct 2020 through: competitive contract → 2yr option → negotiated procedure (Art.32§2γ) after two competitive tenders suspended by preliminary appeals (ΕΣΗΔΗΣ 175970 Mar 2023, ΕΣΗΔΗΣ 195241 Jun 2023). Four ΕΑΔΗΣΥ consents obtained before award. Procedure has documented legal basis; the late publication is the documentable breach. |

### Finding H2 — UNISON misclassification of negotiated contracts 🔴
| | |
|---|---|
| **What** | 6 UNISON contracts 2023–2026 totalling ~€3.24M recorded under decision type Δ.1 (ΑΝΑΘΕΣΗ ΕΡΓΩΝ/ΠΡΟΜΗΘΕΙΩΝ) — the direct-award type. These are negotiated procedures under Art.32§2γ, which must be recorded as Δ.2.4 (ΑΝΑΘΕΣΗ ΜΕ ΔΙΑΠΡΑΓΜΑΤΕΥΣΗ) |
| **Legal basis** | Art. 38 Law 4412/2016 + Diavgeia classification obligations |
| **Referral to** | **ΕΑΑΔΗΣΥ** (classification compliance) |
| **Note** | The misclassification inflates the hospital's ΑΝΑΘΕΣΗ (Δ.1) count and amounts — it does not change the substance of the contracts |

### Finding H3 — ΙΝΤΕΡΚΑΤ: sole-bidder H/M maintenance (two consecutive tenders) 🔴
| | |
|---|---|
| **What** | ΑΦΟΙ ΠΑΠΑΪΩΑΝΝΟΥ ΑΤΕΒΕ (ΙΝΤΕΡΚΑΤ, AFM 999917261) has won the hospital's electromechanical maintenance contract (CPV 50710000-5) as the **only remaining bidder** in two successive open competitive tenders: ΕΣΗΔΗΣ 95109 (Nov 2021, €1.4M/2yr) and ΕΣΗΔΗΣ 345751 (Nov 2024, €2.46M/3yr). Incumbent since at least 2018. |
| **Amount** | €820k/year excl. VAT under current contract |
| **Legal basis** | Art. 107 Law 4412/2016 (obligation to ensure genuine competition); ΙΝΤΕΡΚΑΤ qualifies only via borrowed expertise (Art.78) — primary activity is quarrying/mining (ΓΕΜΗ 14003556000, zero H/M maintenance KADs) |
| **Referral to** | **ΕΑΑΔΗΣΥ** (tender specification review — whether criteria preclude new entrants); **ΕΣ** (9-year incumbent, no price competition) |
| **Supporting ADAs** | `6ΕΖΟ4690ΒΜ-ΠΩ5` (2021 ΚΑΤΑΚΥΡΩΣΗ); `9ΟΗ84690ΒΜ-Δ5Ε` (2024 ΚΑΤΑΚΥΡΩΣΗ) — both explicitly state "μοναδικός οικονομικός φορέας" |

### Finding H4 — Security staffing increase: 50% FTE expansion, undocumented 🟡
| | |
|---|---|
| **What** | Concurrent security posts increased from 17 FTE (2020 bridge) → 19 FTE (2021 tender) → 28+1 FTE (2023 tender, ADA `6ΒΘ84690ΒΜ-464`). The 2021 revision documentation shows budget was increased first, then the committee was asked to propose headcount consistent with the higher budget — i.e., money drove staffing, not operational need |
| **Cost impact** | Annual rate: ~€361k (2020) → €529k excl. VAT (2024) = **+47% excl. VAT**; per-bed cost **2.9–4.4× peer hospitals** (ΓΝ Τρικάλων ~€145k/yr, ΓΝ Καρδίτσας ~€197k/yr) |
| **Legal basis** | Art. 68 Law 4412/2016 (technical specifications must be proportionate to genuine needs) |
| **Referral to** | **ΕΣ** (expenditure regularity); **ΕΑΑΔΗΣΥ** (specification proportionality review) |
| **Supporting ADAs** | `6ΒΘ84690ΒΜ-464` (2023 Board approval of tender specs) |
| **Note** | Tender has attracted 2 genuine bidders in each cycle (ARGOS, ΗΦΑΙΣΤΟΣ) — the competition concern is specification inflation, not absence of competition |

### Finding H5 — Structural: ΚΑΤΑΚΥΡΩΣΗ inversion vs peer hospital 🟡

## ENTITY 2: Δήμος Λαμίας (Municipality)
*org=6166 · 2020–May 2026 · 10,388 ΑΝΑΘΕΣΗ (16.1% of all decisions)*

### Finding M1 — ΚΟΥΤΚΙΑΣ: above-threshold security, no competitive tender 🔴
| | |
|---|---|
| **What** | Building security guard services awarded directly above the €30,000 net legal threshold in consecutive years, with no competitive tender ever published for this service (2020–2026) |
| **Amounts & ADAs** | 2020: €24,435 + €24,435 same day (ADA 8 Apr 2020); 2022: €73,999 ΚΟΥΤΚΙΑΣ & ΣΙΑ ΕΕ AFM 801089930 (`6ΙΕΠΩΛΚ-05Η`) + €73,999 ΚΟΥΤΚΙΑΣ Παν. AFM 112437726 (`ΨΜΛΔΩΛΚ-ΘΙ0`) — same service, same year, two entities; 2023: €74,400 (`ΡΒΥΘΩΛΚ-Τ4Κ`). **Total identified: ≥€222k** |
| **Legal basis** | Art. 118 § 1 Law 4412/2016: above €30,000 net requires competitive procedure. Art. 6 § 2: prohibition on splitting to avoid thresholds (2022 dual award is same service split between family entities) |
| **Referral to** | **ΕΑΑΔΗΣΥ** (direct-award above threshold); **Εισαγγελέας** if deliberate splitting confirmed |
| **Peer context** | Zero above-threshold security direct awards at any peer municipality (Τρίκαλα, Καρδίτσα, Κοζάνη) |

### Finding M2 — ΚΑΝΑΒΕΤΑΣ-ΚΑΡΑΔΗΜΑΣ ΟΕ: geographic contract fragmentation 🔴
| | |
|---|---|
| **What** | AFM 800458127 (ΓΕΜΗ 123643354000, electrical materials retailer, Lamia) received 34 direct awards totalling **€420,989** (2020–2025). Awards systematically split by Δημοτική Ενότητα to keep each below threshold, with same-day multi-ΔΕ clusters explicitly showing single purchases being split |
| **Most egregious cluster** | 2023-03-01/06: 4 awards in 6 days across 4 ΔΕ (Λαμίας, Γοργοποτάμου, Υπάτης, Λιανοκλαδίου), combined **€79,990** = 2.7× the €30k threshold |
| **Legal basis** | Art. 6 § 2 Law 4412/2016: "απαγορεύεται η κατάτμηση δημόσιας σύμβασης με σκοπό την αποφυγή εφαρμογής των διατάξεων του παρόντος νόμου." Geographic splitting by ΔΕ is explicitly addressed |
| **Referral to** | **ΕΑΑΔΗΣΥ**; **Εισαγγελέας** (systematic, multi-year, deliberate pattern) |

### Finding M3 — ΖΗΚΑ & ΚΑΙΛΑΣ: CPV misclassification on above-threshold awards 🔴
| | |
|---|---|
| **What** | Two above-threshold direct awards using CPV 85312320-8 (Social counselling services, Annex XIV light-touch regime), but the 2024 contract (`6ΕΔ0ΩΛΚ-0ΤΖ`, €74,400) is explicitly for "publicity and evaluation services for the Community Centre" — not social counselling |
| **Legal basis** | Art. 118 Law 4412/2016 (threshold); misapplication of Annex XIV exception |
| **Referral to** | **ΕΑΑΔΗΣΥ** (classification abuse); 2022 contract requires legal assessment before referral |

### Finding M4 — Events/cultural spending: 4.9× peer rate, near-zero competitive tendering 🟡
| | |
|---|---|
| **What** | 1,210 direct awards for cultural events and civic programming (2020–2026), vs 67–247 at comparable municipalities. Only 2 competitive tenders in 6 years for this category. No above-threshold individual awards (max €36,754). 253 unique suppliers — no concentration |
| **Nature** | Administrative process failure, not directed spending. Each event component individually awarded instead of framework agreements for recurring services |
| **Referral to** | Background context; supports systemic referral to **ΕΔΑ** for administrative audit |

### Finding M5 — ΜΑΝΤΕΣ-ΤΣΑΜΗΣ ΟΕ: annual threshold-ceiling IT awards 🟡
| | |
|---|---|
| **What** | 6 direct awards at exactly the legal maximum (€37,196–37,200) in consecutive years (2022–2025) for IT services, alongside legitimate competitive awards. Pattern satisfies Art.6§2 criteria if awards cover continuing annual services |
| **Referral to** | **ΕΑΑΔΗΣΥ** for further examination; requires procurement-intent documentation |
