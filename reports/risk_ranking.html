Common pattern: why this matters

Hospital cleaning is a predictable, recurring need with well-established market competition in Greece (multiple companies compete at scale). There is no legitimate basis for invoking the emergency negotiated procedure (Art. 32 Dir. 2014/24/EU) for a routine annual service contract. When the same company is continuously renewed via emergency extension rather than open tender:
  • The hospital avoids the competition that would force pricing downward
  • The incumbent company faces no competitive pressure to improve service or price
  • Each extension resets the clock and prevents any challenger from participating
  • The cumulative value (€3.26M + €2.36M + €5.8M+ across 3 hospitals) far exceeds the EU publication threshold
This pattern should be referred to ΕΑΔΗΣΥ (procurement oversight authority) and the relevant hospital boards.
