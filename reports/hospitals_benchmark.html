🏥 Hospital Diavgeia Benchmark
Greece Procurement Intelligence · 7 NHS Hospitals · 2019–2026
Generated 2026-06-02
What this report shows: Each Greek public hospital must publish all administrative decisions on Diavgeia. This benchmark compares the composition of those publications across 7 NHS hospitals. Because hospitals use different internal workflows — and some route large-volume procurement through the central purchasing body ΕΠΥ — the same economic activity may appear under different decision types, or not appear at all. Direct comparison requires understanding publication practice, not just record counts.
📋 Decision Type Glossary
ΑΝΑΘΕΣΗ Direct award — procurement without competitive tender. Cap: €30k (Art.118) or Art.32 emergency exception. Primary fraud-risk indicator.
ΚΑΤΑΚΥΡΩΣΗ Competitive tender finalization — winner selected after open/restricted procedure. Compliant procurement path.
ΣΥΜΒΑΣΗ Contract signing — may follow a tender or a direct award.
ΠΕΡΙΛΗΨΗ ΔΙΑΚΗΡΥΞΗΣ Tender notice — competitive call publication.
ΑΝΑΛΗΨΗ ΥΠΟΧΡΕΩΣΗΣ Budget commitment. Amounts unreliable (year-end batch entries, encoding errors).
ΟΡΙΣΤΙΚΟΠΟΙΗΣΗ ΠΛΗΡΩΜΗΣ Payment finalization — actual invoice payment. Amount reflects real expenditure.
ΕΠΙΤΡΟΠΙΚΟ ΕΝΤΑΛΜΑ Older payment-order format, used heavily by ΓΝ ΤΡΙΚΑΛΑ.
ΚΑΝΟΝΙΣΤΙΚΗ ΠΡΑΞΗ Regulatory/governance decision — not procurement. Dominant in ΓΝ ΚΑΡΔΙΤΣΑ.
⚠️ Data Quality & Comparability Notes
ΕΠΥ Routing (ΓΝ ΚΑΡΔΙΤΣΑ, ΓΝ ΛΑΡΙΣΑ): 0 ΑΝΑΘΕΣΗ records. Procurement routed through ΕΠΥ (Επιτροπή Προμηθειών Υγείας, AFM 999336270) and published under that org-ID. These hospitals' own feeds reveal nothing about their procurement.
ΑΝΑΛΗΨΗ amounts corrupted: ΓΝΑ ΕΥΑΓΓΕΛΙΣΜΟΣ = €10B+ (capped), ΓΝ ΑΜΦΙΣΣΑ = €369M for a 120-bed hospital. All ΑΝΑΛΗΨΗ amounts are excluded from monetary comparisons.
ΓΝΑ ΕΥΑΓΓΕΛΙΣΜΟΣ 2024 spike: €60.6M of the €60.7M total ΑΝΑΘΕΣΗ appears in 2024 only (zero in all other years). Almost certainly a data reclassification event, not actual procurement. Excluded from amount comparisons.
Coverage: ΓΝΑ ΑΧΕΠΑ and ΓΝΑ ΕΥΑΓΓΕΛΙΣΜΟΣ data starts 2019; all others start 2020.

Key Profile Observations
ΓΝ ΛΑΜΙΑ: 11.6% ΑΝΑΘΕΣΗ — highest direct-award share. 5,887 direct-award records visible in Diavgeia over 7 years. High transparency but also high risk exposure.
ΓΝ ΤΡΙΚΑΛΑ: 2.9% ΚΑΤΑΚΥΡΩΣΗ + 28.1% ΕΠΙΤΡΟΠΙΚΟ ΕΝΤΑΛΜΑ (older payment format). Unique publication profile. Most active competitive-tender user in cohort.
ΓΝ ΚΑΡΔΙΤΣΑ: 31.8% ΚΑΝΟΝΙΣΤΙΚΗ ΠΡΑΞΗ — unusually high regulatory-decision share. 0% procurement. Procurement completely hidden from their own feed.
ΓΝΑ ΑΧΕΠΑ: 59% ΟΡΙΣΤΙΚΟΠΟΙΗΣΗ (25,941 payment records, €266.8M) — highest payment transparency. Confirms large actual procurement volume despite ΕΠΥ routing of some categories.

Interpretation: What the Ratios Mean
ΓΝ ΤΡΙΚΑΛΑ (ratio 0.11 — best): For every 1 direct award, 9 competitive tenders are run. This is the legally expected model. However a T4 amendment-inflation pattern is suspected: Contract 10/2025 amended to €800k → Contract 49/2026 starts at exactly €800k.
ΓΝ ΛΑΜΙΑ (ratio 14.5 — worst with valid data): For every competitive tender finalization, 14.5 direct awards are issued. ISS HELLAS/UNISON (AFM 094081864) receives emergency Art.32§2γ cleaning awards repeatedly for a routine service — €3.26M across 8 decisions.
ΓΝΑ ΑΧΕΠΑ (ratio 7.9): ΑΧΟΥΤΟΣ ΕΠΙΣΙΤΙΣΜΟΣ has received emergency cleaning contracts every year since 2019 (€8.16M, 46 decisions). A service running continuously for 7 years cannot legitimately qualify as an "emergency".
ΓΝΑ ΕΥΑΓΓΕΛΙΣΜΟΣ (ratio 3,372:1 — unreliable): Routes most procurement through ΕΠΥ. The ratio reflects publication practice. 2024 amount spike (€60.6M) requires investigation at ADA level.
